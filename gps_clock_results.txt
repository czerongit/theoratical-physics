GPS Clock Analysis Results
================================================================================

THEORETICAL FRAMEWORK
----------------------------------------
From Embedding Evolution Theorem:
  c ~ K_min^(1/2)
  Δc/c = (1/2) × ΔK_min/K_min

Local curvature couples to gravity:
  ΔK_min/K_min ≈ Δφ/c²

Therefore:
  Δc/c = (1/2) × Δφ/c² (geometric effect)
  Δf/f = Δφ/c² (standard GR redshift)

The geometric effect is ~50% of GR, not a tiny correction!

CONSTANTS
----------------------------------------
K_min = H_0/c = 7.272e-27 m^-1
H_0 = 2.180e-18 s^-1
c = 2.998e+08 m/s

----------------------------------------
To detect geometric c-variation:
  - Effect size: ~2.5×10^-10 at GPS altitude

The geometric effect is detectable with current optical clocks!
